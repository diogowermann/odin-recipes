Apple Pie

Introduction

If you're looking for the best homemade apple pie recipe of all time, you've come to the right place. Grandma Ople's recipe is one of our most popular recipes ever. Try it today to learn what all the hype is about!

Ingredients

  • 6 large apples
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 pie crust (top and bottom)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Peel and slice the apples, then mix with sugar, cinnamon, and flour.
  3. Line a pie dish with one crust, add the apple mixture, and dot with butter.
  4. Cover with the second crust, seal the edges, and make small slits on top for ventilation.
  5. Bake for 50 minutes or until golden brown. Cool before serving.
