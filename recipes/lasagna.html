Lasagna

Introduction

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef, then add marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake for 45 minutes. Cool before serving.
