Cheese Cake

Introduction

Looking for the best recipe for cheesecake on the internet? Well, you've found it! This easy cheesecake (from Philadelphia, the iconic cream cheese brand) will find a permanent home in your recipe box.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 packs of cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter; press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Pour the mixture over the crust and bake for 50 minutes.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
